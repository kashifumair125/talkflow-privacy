Privacy Policy for TalkFlow

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

TalkFlow ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Conversation Text: The messages you paste into TalkFlow are sent to Google's Gemini AI for processing. We do NOT store these messages.
  • Saved Replies: Replies you choose to save are stored locally on your device only.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, crash reports
  • Advertising Data: Ad views and interactions (via Google AdMob)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-generated conversation suggestions
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To analyze app usage and fix bugs

4. Data Storage

Important: We do NOT store your conversations on our servers. Your conversation data is:

  • Sent directly to Google Gemini AI for processing
  • Not saved or logged by TalkFlow
  • Processed in real-time and immediately discarded

Only your saved favorite replies are stored locally on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

5.1 Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: Generate conversation suggestions
  • Data shared: Your conversation text (temporarily)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

5.2 Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display advertisements
  • Data collected: Device info, ad interactions
  • Privacy Policy: AdMob Privacy Policy
  • You can opt out: Ads Settings

6. Data Security

We take your privacy seriously:

  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No conversation data is stored on our servers
  • Saved replies are stored locally on your device
  • We do not sell or share your personal information

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access data stored on your device
  • Delete saved replies from the app
  • Opt out of personalized ads
  • Uninstall the app (removes all local data)

8. Children's Privacy

TalkFlow is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has used our app, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Data Retention

  • Conversation text: NOT stored (processed in real-time only)
  • Saved replies: Stored locally until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Usage data: Retained by third-party services per their policies

11. International Users

TalkFlow is available globally. By using the app, you consent to your data being processed according to this policy, regardless of your location.
